Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information Collection

This service requires no registration or login and collects no personal information. The server retains only standard access logs (IP addresses, timestamps, request paths) for operational troubleshooting.

Query Data

Domain queries you initiate are forwarded to public WHOIS/RDAP servers. We may briefly cache results to improve performance. We do not associate query keywords with user identity, nor do we use query history for analytics or profiling.

Local Storage

Your custom TLD list is saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

Results originate from public domain registries. We do not share your usage data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking scripts.

Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon publication.
